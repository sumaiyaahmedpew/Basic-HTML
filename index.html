Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a small and beautiful country in South Asia. We got independence in 1971 from Pakistan after a long war. Because of the sacrifice of millions of freedom fighters we got this country. 16 December is our Victory Day because this day the Pakistani army surrendered.

Quota MOvement

The quota movement in Bangladesh, primarily driven by students, initially aimed to reform the quota system for government jobs, advocating for a merit-based system. The movement escalated after the government's response to the protests, particularly the July 2024 massacre, which led to widespread public anger and a broader anti-government uprising, known as the July Revolution.
